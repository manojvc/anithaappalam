About Anitha Appalam

Anitha Appalam is a traditional appalam maker based in Jaihindpuram, Madurai. We produce a wide range of crispy appalams — Classic, Pepper, Poo, Sovi, Kalyana, Leaf, and Greens Appalam — using time-tested recipes and carefully sourced ingredients.

Every batch is prepared hygienically, sun-dried to the right texture, and packed with care. We supply to households, shops, caterers, and events across Madurai and surrounding areas. Bulk orders are always welcome.

We also stock Greens Appalam and Ajantha Appalam — established brands trusted across Tamil Nadu for their consistent quality and crunch. Whether you need a small retail pack or a large function order, we have the right product for you.

Anitha Appalam is operated by Visalakshi Traders, located at 89/4 Angayarkanni 2nd Street, Jeeva Nagar, Jaihindpuram, Madurai – 625011, Tamil Nadu. We serve customers from Madurai, Dindigul, Virudhunagar and nearby towns. Call us at +91 98947 00331 or +91 98940 17181 for bulk enquiries and pricing.
